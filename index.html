Your Coding Barkada!

BarkaDevs is your go-to coding barkada, where collaboration meets innovation. We're a tight-knit team of web developers dedicated to supporting and inspiring each other.


Together, we code, collaborate, and elevate our skills, crafting digital solutions that redefine what's possible in the world of web development.

Where friends code together, turning ideas into reality.

At SEAIT, a bunch of us IT students, led by Keyan Andy Delgado, decided to team up and call ourselves BarkaDevs. We all really like learning about web development, and Keyan helps us figure things out. We're like a group of friends who enjoy working together on coding projects, staying up late to solve problems and turn our ideas into actual websites.


As a team, we take pride in turning lines of code into tangible websites, not only for academic excellence but also for the joy of creating something meaningful. BarkaDevs is more than just a group of IT students – it's a community that thrives on collaboration, friendship, and the shared passion for making technology work for us. Joining BarkaDevs means joining a story of learning, growing, and enjoying the exciting challenges that come with being IT students at SEAIT.
